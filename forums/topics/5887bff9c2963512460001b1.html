Hello everyone,

Discord
Before I go ahead and announce the good news, I would like to remind the team leaders to email [email protected] their discord name if they don't have it on their profile. In order to participate in the tournament this is absolutely necessary! Failing to provide this information will result in your team not being able to participate.

Accepted teams
Due to the fact that we had more signups than the maximum of 32 teams, we decided to invite all 35 teams that met the minimum player requirements. And to give the 6 lowest seeded teams the possibility to play in a knockout round on the 27 January at 9:00 am EST. The knockout round will consist of 1 gamemode that will be decided by vetoing. The following teams are invited to play in the knockout round:
Midas will be playing against Amnesia
Frostfire will be playing against Ominous
Chorem will be playing against Cryptossys
